Instrument Utilizations, Lab: {{ lab_analysis.lab.name }}

Computes instrument utilization by hours for a given instrument_name and year. Computes total run hours for each process by multiplying the duration of the process for a fixed sample count by the projectd volume for that assay divided by days per year * the number of samples/day for the process. The total run hours are summed across all processes and this number is divided by the total number of hours = integrated hours * integrated copies of instrument + walkup hours * walkup copies of the instrument.
